Where Ethical leadership Rose

Testimonials

Voices from SSLC 2025

Adroni Rodney, Speaker of the National Youth Parliament and a facilitator at the conference,We officiated the launch of the Sembabule Students leaders Conference 2025 under the theme " Unleashing the Next Generation of Ethical Leaders." The conference brought together students from across Sembabule District to be nurtured in ethical leadership values. Addressing the challenge of rampant corruption in the nation, the Soar mentorship emphasized instilling compassion and integrity among young leaders. We affirm the commitment of holding more such engagements and I express gratitude to SoarAway Africa and illumination initiative for championing this initiative to nurture the next generation of ethical leaders.

Adroni Rodney

8th,Speaker of the National Youth Parliament
